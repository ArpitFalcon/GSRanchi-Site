GirlScript Ranchi

GirlScript is a non- profit organization registered under Government of India. It's aim is to help beginners in tech and support equality in tech. GirlScript Ranchi is the very first Ranchi Chapter of the GirlScript foundation. Come along! Join us and become a part of India's fastest growing tech community. Let us learn together and grow together.
